Story

Alone...
I won but, however, I failed my mission - to save the world. After the final invasion wave was stopped and the mob was pushed back something happened that a whole world paid with their lives. Inside the wormhole a big explosion took place which forced the wormhole to collapse and erased the whole solar system. I could only escape with the help of the my jump-drive. But that used the whole recourses of my ship and now I drift alone through the void.

The thrust-drives are still able to work and therefore there's only one logical way out.. I have to return! Perhaps there's something in the expanse of rubble, which I can use to maximize the energy of my ship. Then I could jump to the next base and the madness would have an end.

Description

Asteroids bounce against each other... and the players, too. Sometimes there are gravitation influences. With it you can make unusual manoeuvres.
