Briefly info

Release data: 11 September, 2000
Platform: Windows

Story

Sometime in the future... The human race has colonised new planet systems with the usage of artificial wormholes. The old earth is uninhabitable and the human race was forced to find a 'new' earth. They found a planet and lived there for a long time in harmony with all other species. But one Race got bored and so they started a war to have a something to do and their main target was the new earth of the mankind. At last their troops were seen entering the wormhole to the earth and all human troops are in peace missions....

Description

You control a spaceship you could fire and you have to preserve your planet... a invasions of aliens which comes thought a wormhole is running. Bang, Bang... till the Big Boss comes!
