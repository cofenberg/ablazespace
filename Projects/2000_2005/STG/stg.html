Briefly info

Release date: 13 October, 2002
Platform: Windows

Description

'Softgames.de - The Game' is like the name says a promotion game for Softgames.de.
You control the Softgames.de mascot 'Url' through an over the water hovering, random generated comic world. Your main mission is to supply testers which are spread over the world, collect crystals and to get that many points as possible. Bloodthirsty mosquitos and turtles with a bad mood want to kill you. In addition you will need some skill to handle the sluggishly control.

Features

* mp3 music by Boris 'Toxeen' Nonte
* sound-effects & music via Fmod (V3.6)
* 3D-graphic via OpenGL
* steerage via DirectInput V8.0
* different languages (could be extended)
* comic look via cel-shading
* Cheats
