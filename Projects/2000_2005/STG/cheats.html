Cheats for Softgames.de - The Game

You could start the game with this parameter:

Parameter This happens
-cheats Activates the cheats
-immortal You couldn't die and you couldn't fall down
-nomosquitos Deactivates the mosquitos
-noturtles Deactivates the turtles
-unlimitedlives You have unlimited lives
-noscoredecrease No time points will be taken
-notimelimit No time limit
-quick Less game elements
-freecamera With Strg-C you chould change the camera mode
