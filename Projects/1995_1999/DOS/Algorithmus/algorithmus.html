Briefly info

Release data: 26 March, 1998
Platform: MS-DOS

Description

When the game begins, a field flashes a few seconds. You have to click on this field. After that two fields flash. Click on both fields in the order they flash. A third field flashes and so on. You have to remember the order. That goes on until you click on a wrong field (inadvertently or deliberately).
